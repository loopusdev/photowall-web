PhotoWall Alpha v0.1

Display you photos in realtime on wall (TV, projector or on any screen with browser inside).

* App is currently in public Alpha testing mode! Any data (photos, wall and user info) can be deleted without notification!

Planed release date for production v1.0 is 1st of January 2015

For testing is recommended using Google Chrome and iOS 7, know issues: Firefox - problem with pushing new photos over sockets, Android - app crashes after using camera and a lot of problems with UI :))

Please send your feedback to photowall@loopus.co

How to test

  1. Use Google Chrome browser
    1. Create wall at photowall.loopus.co
    2. Open wall (in fullscreen)
  2. Download AppGyver Scanner native app
    • iOS
    • Android
  3. PhotoWall mobile app are published at AppGyver X / PhotoWall
    • With AppGyver Scanner scan QR code to open app from AppGyver SHARE:
      https://share.appgyver.com/?id=11940&hash=c36c016ba88ce73aa1b16f3aca24891be80dd3629572355336ac0f3172ed81fe
  4. Take photo with mobile app!
  5. Photos are dinamicaly pushed to wall opened in Google Chrome
